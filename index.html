Tool for uploading photos to Glacier and sync it with Google Drive

View on GitHub Download Portable Download Installer for Windows

This tool helps you to store and sync your files across multiple storages: local disk, Amazon Glacier, Google Drive. If you lose files from one storage you always can get their from another.

Also this tool allow you scale your images to smaller dimensions and save space on your Google Drive. You can store original photos on Amazon Glacier and use Google Drive for view and share your photos.
